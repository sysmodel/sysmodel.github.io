Hadi Jalali, PhD - Postdoctoral Fellow - MITACS Fellow


Dr. Hadi Jalali is an experienced mechanical engineer and scholar with experience working in oil & gas and aerospace. He completed his PhD in the area of multi- physics modelling and simulation and structural dynamics at Memorial University of Newfoundland with applications to cable structures. He is currently a postdoctoral fellow and team leader in Sys-MoDEL in Vehicle Dynamics and Control in partnership with our industrial collaborator, Potential Motors.
