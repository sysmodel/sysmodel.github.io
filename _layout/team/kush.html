Kush Bubbar, PhD, P.Eng - Assistant Professor


Dr. Kush Bubbar is an Assistant Professor at the University of New Brunswick, jointly appointed between Electrical and Computer Engineering and the Dr. J. Herbert Smith Centre for Technology Management and Entrepreneurship. Kush is the Director of Sys-MoDEL and has expertise in the modelling, simulation, control, and design of complex technical systems. Prior to his appointment at UNB, Kush held senior engineering leadership roles at iconic Canadian technology development firms SCIEX™ and BlackBerry™.
